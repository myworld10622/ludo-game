01 🎲

Ludo Basics

Ludo ek real multiplayer game hai jahan players table join karke apas me match khelte hain. Tournament mode me aapka goal hota hai ki aap round by round match jeet kar aage badho aur final winners me aao.

👤 2 Player Match
Aapka match ek aur real player ke saath hota hai. Winner next round me jata hai.
👥 4 Player Match
4 real players ek table par hote hain. Qualified players next round me ja sakte hain.
⚡ Knockout Format
Ek round haarne par aap tournament se bahar ho sakte ho. Har match important hota hai.
💡
Simple baat: Tournament me register karna alag step hai aur actual match khelna alag step hai. Pehle aap tournament join karte ho, phir jab time aata hai tab table ya match join karte ho.

Match Types at a Glance

Format Players Per Match Advancement Risk
1v1 2 Winner advances Medium
4-Player 4 Top players qualify High
Knockout 2–4 Lose = eliminated Very High
02 📊

Tournament Status — Kya Matlab Hota Hai

Jab aap Unity app me tournament page open karte ho, to aapko har tournament ka ek status dikhai deta hai. Ye status batata hai ki tournament abhi kahan hai apni lifecycle me.

Status Matlab Aap Kya Kar Sakte Ho
Registration Open Abhi register ho sakta hai ✅ Register karo
Registration Closed Registration band ho gayi 🚫 Join nahi ho sakta
In Progress Tournament chal raha hai ▶️ Play Now (agar registered)
Completed Tournament khatam ho gaya 👁 Details dekho only
Cancelled Tournament cancel ho gaya 💰 Refund milega wallet me
⚠️
Dhyan rakho: Status tab tak update nahi hota jab tak aap page refresh nahi karte ya app me tournament list dobara load nahi karte. Always latest info ke liye refresh karo.
03 🏆

Tournament Join Guide

Jab aap Unity app me Tournament Page open karte ho, to aapko live tournaments ya registration open tournaments dikhte hain. Har tournament ka status alag ho sakta hai.

📝 Registration Open
Aap tournament detail dekh kar register kar sakte ho. Tournament abhi start nahi hua hota.
🔥 In Progress / Running
Agar aap already registered ho aur match available hai, to Play Now se join kar sakte ho.
✅ Completed
Tournament finish ho chuka hota hai. Is stage me naya registration ya play possible nahi hota.

Tournament Details Popup Me Kya Dikhta Hai

  • Tournament Name
  • Status (current lifecycle stage)
  • Entry Fee (wallet se deduct hogi)
  • Prize Pool (total winning amount)
  • Players Count (registered / max)
  • Registration Close Time
  • Tournament Start Time
  • Play Slots (specific time windows)
  • Aapka Current Wallet Balance
  • Register / Play Now Button
✅
Tip: Details popup me entry fee aur prize pool dono compare karo. Agar prize pool entry fee se kaafi zyada hai, to ye ek acha tournament ho sakta hai.
04 📋

Register Kaise Karein

Tournament me join karna ek simple process hai. Niche step-by-step guide hai jo batata hai ki registration kaise hoti hai aur kya expect karo.

1
Tournament List Open Karo
App me Tournament section me jao. Available tournaments ki list dikhegi.
2
Tournament Select Karo
Jo tournament join karna hai uska card ya button click karo. Details popup open hoga.
3
Entry Fee aur Time Check Karo
Entry fee, prize pool, registration close time, aur play slots dhyan se padho.
4
Register Button Press Karo
Popup me Register button press karo. Confirm karo ki aap register karna chahte ho.
5
Entry Fee Deduct Hogi
Agar wallet balance enough hai to entry fee automatically deduct ho jayegi.
6
Registration Successful
Aap tournament me add ho jaoge. Registered status dikhai dega. Ab slot time ka wait karo.
⚠️
Important: Register karne ke baad aapko play slot ya tournament timing ke hisaab se online aana hota hai. Sirf registration kar lena enough nahi hota — aapko actual match time par bhi present rehna padega.
05 ⏰

Play Slot Timing Guide

Kuch tournaments me play slots diye jaate hain. Play slot ka matlab hai specific time window jisme players ko online rehna hota hai, taaki real users ek dusre ke saath match start kar sakein.

📅 Example Slot
01 Apr 2026, 01:05 PM – 01:20 PM
Is time window me aapko available rehna chahiye.
❓ Kyun Zaruri Hai
Jab zyada players same slot me online aate hain to table jaldi fill hoti hai aur real match fast start hota hai.

Play Slot Ke Rules

  • Slot timing tournament details me pehle hi dikhai jaati hai — register se pehle dekh lo
  • Register karne se pehle user ko timing confirm kar leni chahiye ki wo available hai
  • Slot ke time par online rehna user ki apni responsibility hai
  • Multiple slots ho sakte hain — har round ka alag slot ho sakta hai
  • Agar aap slot time par play nahi karte, to aap tournament se Eliminated ho sakte ho
🚨
Warning: Tournament join karne se pehle hamesha slot time dekh lo. Agar aap us time online nahi reh sakte, to tournament join mat karo — entry fee waste hogi.

Slot Time Par Kya Karna Chahiye

1
App Open Karo — Slot time se 5 min pehle app open kar lo.
2
Tournament Me Jao — Registered tournament par jao.
3
Play Now Button Press Karo — Jab match available ho, immediately join karo.
4
Match Complete Karo — Match finish karo bina connection drop hone ke.
06 🛠️

Apna Tournament Create Kaise Karein

User panel me aap apna tournament create kar sakte ho. Ye option un users ke liye hai jo apna real-player tournament chalana chahte hain.

Create Form Me Kya Bharna Hota Hai

📝 Basic Info
Tournament name, type (2-player / 4-player), format (knockout/round-robin), description
👥 Player Setup
Max players allowed, players per match, entry fee amount
📅 Schedule
Registration start time, registration close time, tournament start time, play slots
🏆 Prize Setup
Prize pool split among winners, tournament terms & conditions

Create Tournament Karte Waqt Dhyan Rakhne Wali Baatein

  • Name clear aur simple rakho — players easily samjhenge
  • Entry fee aur prize details sahi aur realistic rakho
  • Registration timing realistic rakho — too short = players miss kar sakte hain
  • Play slots aise rakho jisme users actually available ho sakein (prime time prefer karo)
  • Description me short, clear information do — confusing description se players door ho jaate hain
  • Terms & conditions me important rules clearly likho

Create Ke Baad Kya Hota Hai

1
Form Submit Karo — Sari details fill karke submit karo.
2
Tournament Create Ho Jata Hai — System me tournament entry ban jaati hai.
3
Approval Wait (agar required ho) — Kuch tournaments ko admin approval ki zarurat hoti hai. Tab tak wait karo.
4
Registration Open — Approve hone ke baad players properly join kar paate hain.
5
Live Reports — Registrations, live matches, aur results reports show hone lagti hain.
ℹ️
Note: Ek baar tournament create aur approve hone ke baad, uske basic details change karna possible nahi hota. Isliye form bharte waqt sab kuch ek baar dhyan se check karo.
07 💰

Wallet Rules

Tournament join flow me wallet ka role bahut important hota hai, kyunki entry fee direct wallet se deduct hoti hai. Wallet balance kam hone par registration fail ho sakti hai.

Registration Pe
Entry fee deduct hoti hai
Winning Par
Prize wallet me credit hoti hai
Cancellation Par
Entry fee refund milti hai
Balance Kam Ho
Registration fail ho sakti hai

Wallet Deduction Rules — Detail Me

  • Register karte hi wallet se entry fee deduct hoti hai — confirm se pehle check karo
  • Agar balance kam hai to registration fail ho sakti hai aur error show hoga
  • Successful registration ke baad aap tournament list me Registered status dekh sakte ho
  • Winner banne par prize amount automatically wallet me credit ho sakti hai
  • Tournament cancel hone par entry fee original wallet me refund ho jaati hai
  • Winning wallet aur main wallet alag hote hain — withdrawal rules check karo
✅ Before Registering
Wallet balance check karo. Entry fee se kam ho to pehle wallet top-up karo. Register button press karne se pehle confirm karo.
🏆 After Winning
Prize amount wallet me automatically credit ho jata hai. Winning wallet me credit hone me kuch time lag sakta hai.
💡
Best Practice: Register button press karne se pehle apna wallet balance aur entry fee dono zarur check karo. Popup me wallet balance already shown hota hai — use dekhna mat bhoolo.
08 🎭

Common User Scenarios

Ye section un common situations ke baare me hai jo players frequently face karte hain — aur unka correct response kya hona chahiye.

Scenario 01
Registration open hai, match abhi start nahi hua
Aap details dekh ke register karo. Register ke baad aapko tournament timing ka wait karna hai. Play slot time aane par app open karke Play Now karo.
Scenario 02
Aap already registered ho
Aapko dobara register karne ki zarurat nahi hoti. Aapko bas slot time ya match time par online rehna hota hai. Registered status dikhai dega tournament me.
Scenario 03
Tournament full ho gaya
Agar max players fill ho gaye, to naya registration possible nahi hota. Aap details dekh sakte ho lekin join nahi kar paoge. Agle tournament ka wait karo.
Scenario 04
Tournament running hai, aapka match available
Play Now use karke immediately table join karo. Delay karne par aap apna match slot miss kar sakte ho aur tournament se out ho sakte ho.
Scenario 05
Slot time par online nahi aaye
Agar tournament slot-based hai aur aap assigned timing me online nahi aate, to aap tournament se out ho sakte ho. Entry fee refund nahi hogi is case me.
Scenario 06
4-player tournament me kam players online hain
Aapko apne tournament ke time par online rehna hai. Match start logic tournament rules aur current player availability ke hisaab se decide hota hai. Apna kaam karo — online raho.
✅
Golden Rule: Hamesha details padho → wallet check karo → slot timing confirm karo → match ke time online raho. Isi se aapka tournament experience smooth aur successful rahega.

  • ✅Entry fee aur prize pool compare karo

  • ✅Wallet me enough balance confirm karo
  • ✅Terms & conditions padho
🎮 During Tournament
  • ✅Slot time se 5 min pehle app open karo
  • ✅Stable internet connection ensure karo
  • ✅Play Now immediately press karo jab match available ho
  • ✅Match bich me mat choro
  • ✅Score aur next round schedule track karo
🛠️ Apna Tournament Create Karte Waqt
  • ✅Timing realistic rakho — user availability ke hisaab se
  • ✅Description aur terms clearly likho
  • ✅Entry fee aur prize pool fair rakho
  • ✅Maximum players ka accurate number do
  • ✅Submit se pehle sab kuch ek baar review karo
💰 Wallet Management
  • ✅Regular wallet balance check karo
  • ✅Register karne se pehle enough balance confirm karo
  • ✅Winning wallet aur main wallet ka fark samjho
  • ✅Withdrawal rules pehle se padh lo
  • ✅Refund policy samjho tournament cancel hone par
🎯
Final Advice: Ludo tournament me success ka sabse bada factor hai preparation — sahi tournament choose karo, timing confirm karo, aur match ke time fully present raho. Technical gameplay se zyada important hai discipline aur punctuality.
